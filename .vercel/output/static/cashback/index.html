Cashback FAQ

Everything you need to know about our upcoming cashback service.

How does cashback work?

When you shop through Cash Me Up (via our app, browser extension, or member dashboard), we earn a commission from the retailer. We then pass most of that commission back to you as cashback. The retailer pays us — not you — so your cashback is pure profit.

When will Cashback launch?

We're building the cashback platform now. Join free and we'll notify you the moment it goes live. Early members will get access to exclusive launch cashback rates.

Will there be a minimum payout amount?

We're designing the cashback system to pay out with no minimum threshold — meaning every penny you earn can be withdrawn to your bank immediately. Details will be confirmed at launch.

Which retailers will be included?

We're targeting hundreds of UK retailers across groceries, fuel, insurance, fashion, tech, utilities, and more. Our launch lineup will include major supermarkets, online retailers, and insurance comparison sites.

Is there any cost to use cashback?

No. Cash Me Up cashback is completely free to use. There's no subscription, no annual fee, and no percentage taken from your cashback. What we show you is what you get.
