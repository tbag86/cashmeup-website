Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Cash Me Up.

What is Cash Me Up?

Cash Me Up is a free UK platform that helps you find and access money you're entitled to — through compensation claims, government grants, cashback on purchases, and member discounts. You join free, and we match you to opportunities based on your profile.

Is it really free? What's the catch?

Yes, genuinely 100% free — no subscription, no hidden fees, no credit card required. Our business model is based on referral commissions paid by our regulated partners. You never pay anything to use Cash Me Up, ever.

What is a PCP claim and could I be owed money?

A PCP claim relates to mis-sold car finance between 2007 and 2021. Lenders secretly paid dealers hidden commissions that led consumers to pay higher interest than necessary. The regulator found this widespread — if you had PCP or HP car finance during this period, you could be owed £1,000s. It's free to check.

When are the other products (grants, cashback, discounts) launching?

We're actively building each product and will launch them throughout 2026. Join free now and you'll be the very first to know when each one goes live — we'll send you a personal alert.

How is my data protected?

We're fully compliant with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. Your data is stored securely and only shared with regulated partners for the explicit purpose you consented to. We never sell your data to third parties. See our Privacy Policy.

What do I get as a member?

Free membership gives you access to all current and future Cash Me Up products: claims checking, grant matching, cashback, discounts, and priority alerts when new compensation schemes open. It's one free account that unlocks everything we build.
