How are the discounts different from what's already publicly available?

Our discounts will be negotiated exclusively for Cash Me Up members — meaning you won't find them on voucher sites or Google. We're partnering directly with retailers to secure member-only rates that aren't available to the general public.

When does Discounts launch?

We're actively in talks with retailers and building the platform. Join free to get a launch notification — early members will get access to the best introductory deals.

Will I need to pay for access to member discounts?

No. Member discounts are included with your free Cash Me Up account. There is no premium tier, no upgrade cost, and no subscription. Free membership gives you access to everything.

How many retailers will be included at launch?
