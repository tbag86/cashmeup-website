PCP Car Finance Claims

Between 2007 and 2021, millions of UK consumers were charged higher interest rates on PCP and HP car finance because lenders secretly paid dealers hidden commissions. The financial regulator found this practice widespread and potentially unlawful. If you had car finance during this period, you could be owed £1,000s in compensation.

You may qualify if you:

  • Had PCP or HP car finance between 2007 and 2021
  • Finance was arranged through a UK dealership
  • Were a UK resident at the time of the agreement
  • Were not told how your dealer was paid by the lender

Claims FAQ

Common questions about how our claims service works.

What types of claims does Cash Me Up handle?

Currently our live product is PCP and HP car finance claims. We're building claim services for energy mis-selling, bank charges, holiday claims, and mortgage mis-selling — join free to be notified when each one launches.

Is there any cost to make a claim through Cash Me Up?

No. Cash Me Up is completely free to use. We earn a referral commission from our regulated claims management partners — you never pay anything. If your claim is unsuccessful, nobody is owed anything.

How long do claims take?

It depends on the claim type and lender. PCP claims are currently subject to a regulator-led industry review. Our regulated partners will keep you updated throughout the process.

Do I need any documents to start?

No. You don't need to dig out old paperwork. Our partners have the tools to trace your agreements using your basic details. Simply fill in the form and they handle the rest.

Who handles my claim?

All claims are handled by fully regulated, FCA-authorised claims management companies. Cash Me Up acts as an introducer — we connect you with the right experts for your type of claim.

What happens if my claim is unsuccessful?

Absolutely nothing. There is no fee, no charge, no obligation. Our "no win, no fee" promise means if you don't win, you don't pay — and neither do we.
