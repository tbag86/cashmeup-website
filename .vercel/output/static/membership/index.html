Membership FAQ

Everything you need to know about your free Cash Me Up account.

What does free membership actually include?

Free membership gives you access to everything Cash Me Up builds — claims access (PCP live now), grant matching, cashback, member discounts, and smart alerts. There is no paid tier, no premium upgrade, and no catch. Everything we build is included with your free account.

Will membership ever cost money?

No. Cash Me Up's free membership is free forever. Our business model is built on referral commissions and partner fees — never on charging consumers. The free membership promise is permanent.

How do I join?

Fill in the signup form on this page or click "Join Free" anywhere on the site. It takes under a minute — just your name, email, and optional phone number. No payment details required.

What happens after I join?

You'll get an immediate welcome email with access to live products (PCP claims checking right now). As we launch each new product, we'll send you a personal notification. Your member dashboard will fill up with opportunities matched to your profile.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, at any time. Email us at hello@cashmeup.co.uk and we'll delete your account and all associated data within 30 days, in line with our GDPR obligations. You can also unsubscribe from emails at any time using the link in any email we send.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We're fully UK GDPR compliant. Your data is stored securely, never sold to third parties, and only shared with our regulated partners for the specific purpose you've agreed to. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
