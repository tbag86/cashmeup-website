Grants FAQ

Common questions about our upcoming grant matching service.

What types of grants will Cash Me Up help me find?

We're building a comprehensive matching system covering UK government grants for home improvements (insulation, heating, solar), energy efficiency, boiler replacement, business start-up, disability adaptations, cost-of-living support, and more. Join free to be notified at launch.

Are the grants really free money?

Yes. Government grants are funds you don't have to pay back. They're designed to help specific groups of people with specific needs — the challenge is knowing they exist and qualifying for them. That's exactly what Cash Me Up will solve.

How will Cash Me Up match me to grants?

When you join, you'll complete a profile including your home ownership status, income, location, employment, and any disabilities or health conditions. Our system then cross-references this against every available grant scheme and shows you the ones you're eligible for.

Is Cash Me Up's grant matching free?

Yes, completely free. Cash Me Up is always free for consumers. We earn referral fees from service providers and applicants, never from grant applicants.
