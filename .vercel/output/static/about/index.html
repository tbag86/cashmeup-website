Our Business Model ‚Äî Explained Honestly

We believe in being completely transparent about how we make money, so there's no ambiguity whatsoever.

When you submit a PCP claim enquiry through Cash Me Up, we pass your details to one of our regulated claims management partners. If you proceed with a claim and it's successful, the claims management company earns a success fee (a percentage of your compensation, agreed with you upfront). Cash Me Up receives a referral commission from that claims company.

To be absolutely clear: the referral commission we receive comes entirely from our partner, not from you. Your compensation is not reduced by our referral commission. You receive the full agreed amount, minus only the claims company's success fee (which you agree to before proceeding).

As we expand into grants, cashback, and discounted shopping, we'll earn in similar ways ‚Äî from partnerships with providers, not from consumers. The consumer always gets the full benefit.

Questions About Cash Me Up

How does Cash Me Up make money if it's free for consumers?

We operate a referral model. When we pass your details to a regulated claims management company and you proceed with a claim, we receive a referral commission from that partner. This comes entirely from the claims company ‚Äî not from you. You pay nothing, ever. This model allows us to offer a genuinely free service to consumers while building a sustainable business.

Are Cash Me Up's partners properly regulated?

Yes. We only work with claims management companies that are authorised and regulated by the financial regulator or, for legal work, by the Solicitors Regulation Authority (SRA). You can verify any company's regulatory status on the regulatory Register at .

What is Cash Me Up's relationship with the claims companies?

Cash Me Up is a referral service. We identify consumers who may be eligible for claims and connect them with regulated claims management companies. We are not a claims management company ourselves and do not handle claims directly. Our partners handle all claim submissions, communications with lenders, and FOS escalations.

Is Cash Me Up itself regulated?

As a referral and information service, we operate within the framework of UK consumer protection law and data protection regulations (UK GDPR). Our regulated partners are regulated. We are committed to operating transparently and ethically. Our terms and privacy policy set out our obligations in full.

Will Cash Me Up always be free?

Our core consumer service will always be free. Our business model is based on referral commissions, not consumer fees. We are building a platform that puts money back in consumers' pockets ‚Äî that mission is incompatible with charging consumers. As we expand into grants, cashback, and other services, all of them will be free to use.

How can I trust Cash Me Up with my personal data?

We take data protection extremely seriously. We comply with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. Your data is stored securely, used only for the purposes you consent to, and never sold to third parties. We only share your data with our regulated partners in order to process your enquiry. You can read our full Privacy Policy for complete details.
